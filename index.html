About Us

Vision

  • To be the leading environmental consultancy firm that responds to the developers needs and that resonates with the national development agenda.

Mission

  • Seeks to help the developers meet the minimum environmental requirements fast and protects them from developmental challenges and intimidation.

OBJECTIVES

  • To conduct environmental impact assessment and audits/ Social environmental impacts and strategic environmental assessment.
  • To protect the clients from intimidation and harassment through environmental litigation.
  • To research, educate and conserve the environment.
  • To connect developers and development opportunities.

Core Values

  • Professionalism
  • Result oriented
  • Honesty
  • Ethical
  • Confidentiality
  • Commitment and responsive
